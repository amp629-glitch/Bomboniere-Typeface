Capitalics Type Foundry

Capitalics Warsaw Type Foundry is the very first foundry in Poland. With a passion for good typography, a group of typedesigners from the capital city of Warsaw, Poland decided to join forces and create a unique library of typefaces. The ever-growing graphics market requires a special approach. Our offer is addressed to conscious designers seeking original and technologically advanced fonts.

We're a team of type and graphic designers based in Warsaw, Poland passionate about fonts and typography. Our main work involves making unique typefaces and offering custom services like bespoke fonts and typographic consulting.

About the Artist & Founder

Michał Jarociński

One of few professional type designers in Poland, graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw. From 2007, he has been working under the name Dada Studio, making websites and fonts. Michał is the originator of the Capitalics Warsaw Type Foundry project. He supervises the whole project and cares about the high level of typefaces.
