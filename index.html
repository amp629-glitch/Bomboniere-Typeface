Bomboniere Typeface

By Capitalics

Learn More!
The Capitalics Bomboniere font is an elegant display serif font family designed with high contrast and delicate, graceful letterforms. It serves as inspiration for projects requiring a touch of elegance and sophistication, particularly for vintage or modern designs like wedding invitations, formal announcements, and branding.
Whether you're a professional designer looking for the perfect font for a client project, or an individual looking to add a touch of sophistication to your personal projects, Bomboniere is the perfect choice. So why wait? Add this beautiful font to your collection today and elevate your designs to the next level.
But Bomboniere isn't just about good looks - it's also highly functional. It comes in multiple weights and includes a full set of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and punctuation marks.
This font is very expansive, having 10 different weights in both regular and italic. Giving the typface a total of 20 different looks, making it extremely versatile for many displays.
